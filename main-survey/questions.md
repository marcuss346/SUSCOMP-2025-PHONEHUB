## Questions

### Main questionaire

How old are you?
- Short form answer

What is your gender?
- Multiple choice: Female, Male, I don't want to answer.

Which faculty are you studying at? (Students of interdisciplinary programs that take place at multiple faculties, please select any faculty.)
- Multiple choice: Faculty of Mathematics and Physics, Faculty of Computer Science and Informatics

At what level of study are you currently?
- Multiple choice: Undergraduate study, Postgraduate study (Master's), Doctoral study (Doctorate)

What brand of phone are you currently primarily using?
- Multiple choice: Samsung, Apple, Xiaomi, Google, Oneplus, Oppo, Realme

What was the price of the phone from the previous question at the time of purchase?
- Multiple choice

What year did you buy your current primary phone?
- Multiple choice

When do you intend to replace your current primary phone?
- Multiple choice

Why did you replace your previous phone? (If you are not completely sure, please state the most likely reason.)
- Multiple choice

What did you do with your previous phone after the replacement?
- Multiple choice

How many used phones do you currently have stored at home?
- Multiple choice

Why did you choose this method of disposing of the used device? (Select all that apply.)
- Multiple answers can be chosen

Have you heard of waste electrical and electronic equipment (so-called e-waste) before taking this survey?
- Multiple choice

Which of the listed items, in your opinion, fall under e-waste?
- Multiple answers can be chosen
